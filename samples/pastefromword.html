Paste from Word Documentation

To test how Paste from Word works, just download the sample Word document, open it in Microsoft Word, copy content inside and paste into CKEditor. Check clean HTML source code generated by CKEditor using the Source button.

If you would like to test more styles and formatting, download a sample Word document with different styles and formatting and try to paste its content into CKEditor.

Keep in mind that pasted content may be filtered by additional ACF rules. The content appearance in CKEditor also depends on styling used in content.css files.
